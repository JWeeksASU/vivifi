Frequently Asked Question's

What does Vivify mean?
Vivify-to enliven or animate. We want to give life to your event with ASU's fresh and exciting talent.
Why Vivifi?
Our goal is to bring ASU's talent to the event world in Phoenix. "Student" doesn't have to mean lower quality.
Our team of ASU student musicians:
  • Give hundreds of performances a year
  • Have passed rigorous school of music auditions, further being qualified through Vivifi's audition process
  • Have more education and training than most working live musicians
  • Bring a fresh exciting style thatâ€™s hard to find elsewhere
How do I figure out what type of music is best for my event?
You can have the experts take care of the selection through:
  • Vivi-Book

  • Search Artists

How are the musicians qualified?
All musicians are working towards or have a degree from the prestigious ASU School of Music. Additionally, we select the best musicians through a thorough audition process that tests their solo, ensemble, reading, and improvisation skills.
ASU Musicians are talented, but are they reliable?
As part of our screening process we make sure musicians are not only qualified, but are communicative, responsible and punctual. The musicians are so confident in their reliability that if they are late to your event they will play for free.
Do you take special song requests?
Absolutely. If it's not in our set list, the first song request is free and additional song requests are priced based on time needed to learn.
I want an uncommon group/musician and can't find it. What do I do?
Contact us and we'll find it for you!
Who do I contact for last minute questions, concerns or changes?
Contact us by phone for immediate response to any time sensitive issue.
